How to use?

Expression for operations

x(base for x) [+ - * /] y(base for y) = ? (base for result)

For example

137(8) + 1f(16) = ?(7)
Will convert both of the numbers to base 7, add them and then display the result.



Expression for conversions

x(base for x) = ? (base for result)

For example

123(4)= ?(5)
Will convert 123 from base 4 to base 5 and display the result.
